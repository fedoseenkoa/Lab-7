"French Exit" About the album
“French Exit” is the debut studio album by indie pop band TV Girl, self-released June 5, 2014 on their Bandcamp and is the follow-up to TV Girl’s EP “Lonely Women”, released in 2013, and full-lenght debut mixtape “The Wild, The Innocent, The TV Shuffle”, released in 2012.
"French Exit" Main theme of the album
As stated in their Bandcamp:
French Exit is TV Girl’s debut album. Depending on how you look at it. 12 songs about lost lust, too much love and not enough.
"French Exit" Unlisted songs
More songs were made for this album, but eventually did not make the final cut. Some of them were released in a 2020 EP called “The Night in Question: French Exit Outtakes”.
"French Exit" Most popular song

The most popular song on French Exit by TV Girl is “Lovers Rock” with a total of 543.1K page views.
